Jay Mark Caplan is an illustrator and graphic artist living in Montreal.

He brings a wealth of brand and production experience to his studio practice, which aims to be simple, playful, and sincere. He is passionate about printed artwork and how it plays a role in our spaces and memories.

His drawings have appeared on bottles for Canadian brewer Collective Arts Brewery and sold-out merchandise for local indie bookstore Librairie Saint-Henri Books. As a strategist and creative director, Jay has worked with Coca-Cola, LEGO, Fido, Bristol-Myers Squibb and more.
